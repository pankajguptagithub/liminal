About

There was no grand arrival — no triumphant moment or claim to purpose. Just a long walk, a few pauses, and the quiet whisper to keep moving. I’ve worn roles — engineer, scribbler, wanderer, witness — but they’ve never held me. What stays is something beneath: a pull toward the unfinished, the not-yet, the quiet pull of becoming. I build, I write, I wait AND I switftly move through the places in between — where order and chaos both leave their marks

This isn’t a showcase. It’s just a shape, forming — one that holds silence as gently as sound

And if you sense uneven edges here, that may very much be the case! I am a mirror with some fog, a path with some bends, a note with a few cracks. But perhaps that’s the point — that art, like being human, is not the absence of flaws, but the tenderness they allow. After all, I am a mere mortal at the end.Victor is temporary. Liminal is permanent. The rest... belongs to something older, vaster, and still becoming
